Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ReplateIQ ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and in using our products and services.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, Email, and Profile Picture (via Google/Firebase Auth).
  • User Content: Photos of food listings and descriptions you upload.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (Zip Code/City) to facilitate local food recovery. We do not track real-time GPS coordinates without explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Connect you with local community members for food sharing.
  • Verify transactions to generate carbon credit certificates.
  • Analyze aggregated, anonymized data to measure environmental impact.

4. Data Sharing

We strictly do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Google Cloud/Firebase (Hosting), Stripe (Payments).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account at any time within the application settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@replateiq.ai.
